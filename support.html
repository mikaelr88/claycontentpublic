App Support

Thanks for using the app! This app is designed to help ceramic artists document and share their work.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, Apple may sync your data between devices.

Do you collect any personal data?

No. The app does not collect personal information, analytics, or tracking data.

How do I back up my work?

We recommend enabling iCloud backups on your device or exporting your data regularly if that feature is available.

Contact

If you need help or want to report a bug, please contact the developer through the App Store listing.
