Ceramics App

A simple, offline-first iOS app for documenting ceramic work over time.

Privacy Policy Terms of Use Support About

What the App Does

This app helps ceramic artists track pieces from early stages through finished work. Each piece can include photos, notes, and progress over time.

Privacy First

The app does not require accounts, does not track users, and does not collect personal data. All content is stored locally on your device. Optional iCloud sync is handled by Apple.

Support Development

The app is free to use. An optional one-time in-app purchase is available for users who wish to support continued development.
